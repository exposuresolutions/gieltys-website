On the Wild Atlantic Way

Where the Road Ends
and the Views Begin

Perched at the edge of Dooagh village — the last stop before Keem Bay, one of Ireland's most beautiful beaches. Gaze out over Clew Bay, Clare Island, Inishturk and Inisbofin from our coffee shop windows.

Open 10am–10pm daily, all year round. Whether you're touring the Wild Atlantic Way, hiking Croaghaun's cliffs, or charging your EV in our car park — Gielty's is your stop.

Three generations of the Gielty family have welcomed visitors through these doors. The decor may change, but the warmth never will. Come as a stranger, leave as a friend. That's the Gielty's way.
