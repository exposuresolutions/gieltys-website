Directions

How to Get Here

From Westport

~45 minutes

Take the N59 west towards Mulranny and Achill Sound. Cross the bridge onto Achill Island, continue through Keel village, and on to Dooagh. Gielty's is on the left as you enter Dooagh.

From Galway

~2.5 hours

Take the N17 north to Knock, then the N5/N60 to Castlebar. From Castlebar, take the N59 west via Westport to Achill. A stunning drive through Connemara and the west coast.

From Dublin

~4 hours

M4 west to Longford, then N5 to Westport. From Westport, follow the N59 to Achill. One of Ireland's most scenic drives â€” well worth the journey.
