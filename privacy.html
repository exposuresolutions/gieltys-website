Privacy Policy

Last updated: 27 March 2026

Who We Are

This website is operated by Gielty's Clew Bay Bar & Restaurant ("Gielty's", "we", "us"), Keelwest, Dooagh, Achill Island, Co. Mayo, F28 F6C1, Ireland. Our digital presence is managed by Exposure Solutions on our behalf.

For any privacy queries, contact us at info@gieltys.com or call (098) 43119.

What Data We Collect

Booking & Contact Forms

When you book a table or submit a contact enquiry, we collect your name, email address, phone number, party size, date preference, and any message you include. This data is processed solely to fulfil your booking or respond to your enquiry.

Job Applications (Recruitment)

When you apply for a job via our /jobs page, we collect:

  • Name, email, phone number, location
  • Role preference, experience summary, cover note
  • CV/resume document
  • Age confirmation (over 18)
  • Optional: consent to share your application with other Achill Island hospitality employers via the Exposure Solutions central recruitment pool

Recruitment data is retained for 12 months from submission and then permanently deleted unless you request earlier deletion.

Central Recruitment Pool

If you opt in to the central recruitment pool, your application data may be shared with other participating Achill Island hospitality employers managed by Exposure Solutions. Each employer can only see candidates who have consented. You can withdraw consent at any time by emailing info@gieltys.com.

Investment Enquiries

If you submit an enquiry via our /invest page, we collect your name, email, phone, investment interest, and timeline. This data is shared with the property owner and their appointed agents for the purpose of facilitating a potential sale. It is not used for marketing.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to understand how visitors use our website. This collects anonymised data including pages visited, time on site, approximate location (country/region level), device type, and referral source. No personally identifiable information is stored by GA4. You can opt out using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Legal Basis for Processing

  • Bookings & contact: Legitimate interest / contractual necessity
  • Recruitment: Consent (you submit the form voluntarily)
  • Central pool: Explicit opt-in consent (separate checkbox)
  • Investment enquiries: Legitimate interest
  • Analytics: Legitimate interest in improving our service

Who Has Access to Your Data

  • Gielty's Bar & Restaurant — for bookings, contact, recruitment
  • Exposure Solutions — our digital agency, processes data on our behalf
  • Google — anonymised analytics data only
  • Other Achill Island employers — only if you opted into the central recruitment pool
  • We do not sell your data to any third party

Your Rights (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Rectification — correct any inaccurate data
  • Erasure — request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restrict processing — limit how we use your data
  • Data portability — receive your data in a structured format
  • Object — object to processing based on legitimate interest
  • Withdraw consent — at any time, without affecting prior processing

To exercise any right, email info@gieltys.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Cookies

This website uses essential cookies required for functionality and Google Analytics cookies for anonymised usage data. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. By using this website you consent to these cookies.

Data Retention

  • Booking data: 6 months
  • Recruitment data: 12 months (or until you request deletion)
  • Investment enquiry data: Until the property sale is completed or 24 months, whichever is sooner
  • Analytics data: 14 months (Google default)

Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption, secure form handling, and access controls. CVs and personal documents are stored in access-controlled storage managed by Exposure Solutions.

Complaints

If you are unsatisfied with how we handle your data, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Data Protection Commission (DPC), 21 Fitzwilliam Square South, Dublin 2, Ireland — www.dataprotection.ie.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent revision.
